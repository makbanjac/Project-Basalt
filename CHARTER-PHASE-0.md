# Project Basalt — Phase 0 Charter

This document is the founders' binding commitment for the first six months of Project Basalt. It states what will be delivered, by whom, and by when. Every milestone has an owner, a deliverable in this repository, and a verification artifact.

This charter is itself a Phase 0 deliverable. Failure to meet a milestone does not collapse the project, but it triggers a written explanation in the public log and a re-baselining of the timeline by consent of the General Circle.

## Failure Modes & Re-baselining

If a milestone slips by more than 30 days, the responsible Circle Lead files a one-page review in the public log covering: what blocked it, what was tried, what the revised target is, and whether downstream milestones are affected. The General Circle reviews and either ratifies the new timeline by consent or escalates to a charter amendment.

The charter is not a wishlist. Repeated unexplained slippage on multiple milestones is a signal to either reduce ambition or admit that one of the founders does not have the bandwidth to deliver — both of which are recoverable, neither of which is recoverable through silence.

## Out of Scope for Phase 0

To prevent scope creep, the following are explicitly **not** Phase 0 deliverables:

- Construction of any permanent structure on the Alpha Site.
- Off-grid energy or water systems.
- Internal currency, credit ledger, or token.
- Marketing or media work beyond the single coordinated outreach in June.
- Recruitment of capital-only members.
- Phase 2 budget quotation.
- A second site.

These belong in Phases 1 or 2. Pulling them forward kills Phase 0 by exhausting founder bandwidth.

## Amendment

This charter is amended by consent of the two founders during the two-founder phase, and by consent of the General Circle thereafter. All amendments tracked in git.
