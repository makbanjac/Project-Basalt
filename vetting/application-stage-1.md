# Vetting — Stage 1 Application Form

The questions below are the canonical Stage 1 form, ready to paste into Tally, Google Forms, or any equivalent tool.

Form working title: **Project Basalt — Application (Stage 1)**

Estimated completion time: **45–75 minutes** for a serious applicant. Stated explicitly on the form.

Privacy note (visible on the form): *Materials submitted are visible only to the Project Basalt Community Circle. We do not share applications externally. Decline rationale is recorded internally and never published.*

## Section 6 — Conflict Scenario

> A real-feeling, structured prompt. We score for reasoning, not for "the right answer."

**Prompt (visible on the form):**

> *It is the second winter at the Alpha Site. Two Full Members, both in the Infrastructure Circle, disagree about whether to spend €2,400 on a second backup generator. Member A argues redundancy is non-negotiable for safety. Member B argues the money should go toward winterizing the well, which has frozen twice. The Circle has tried to consent twice and failed. The General Circle is meeting in three days. You are a Full Member in another Circle but you will be on-site this winter and depend on both systems.*
>
> *In 200–300 words, describe what you would do between now and the General Circle meeting, and why.*

| # | Field | Type | Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| 6.1 | Your response | Long text (200–300 words) | Yes |

## Reviewer-Side Scoring (not visible on the form)

Each reviewer scores on a 1–5 scale across the four vetting criteria. A pass requires ≥3 from both reviewers on **all four** criteria.

| Criterion | Sources |
|---|---|
| Demonstrated competence | §2, §3 |
| Capital readiness | §4 |
| Governance literacy | §5, §6 |
| Conduct & temperament | §6, §8, language and care across the entire form |

Disagreement between reviewers is escalated to a third reviewer; persistent disagreement is escalated to the General Circle.
