## 12. Visit Conclusion

After the visit, the visiting member writes a one-page narrative summary committed as `site/visit-2026-MM-narrative.md` covering:

- Three things that exceeded expectations.
- Three things that disappointed or worried.
- Estimated readiness score (1–10) for Phase 1 use as-is.
- Top three actions required before Phase 1 can begin on this site.
- Recommendation: proceed / proceed with conditions / re-evaluate.

This narrative is the input the General Circle uses to make the Phase 0 → Phase 1 gating decision.
